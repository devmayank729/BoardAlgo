BoardAlgo is a SAAS platform for 10th grade students to get improvement in their acedmics 

The main features are : 
1) Find the solution (can uplaod photo/type here directly)
    a) Solution will be provided by GeminiAPI 
    b) Most relevent answer from topper's answer sheet will also be shown
    c) Probability that this will come or not 

2) Concept predictor 
    a)Can enter the name of the concept and it will give the probability that it will come or not based on previous year analysis
    b)Give top 10 concepts form this chapter 


3)Personalized question bank 
    a)based on previous conversation, give me the question bank for me 
        i)can solve it here live 
            a)Can type here 
            b)Can upload the solution here 

        ii)can print it as pdf, with the answer sheet or without answer sheet 



4) personalized plan (with constrains like time,concept and chats)

5)Dashboard for parents and students 


6) Board examinor (write the question here directly or upload the photo here)
    a) Get it evaluated (strictly according to CBSE marking scheme)
    b)get suggestion,area of improvement, help in making the personalized question bank 


7) Learn it easily 
    a) Convert any tough mugging up part into mnemonics (like Hlina ki Rb se Fryaad)
    b) Convert any tough concept -------into Story which will improve the remembering part 




    PM Advice: Don't just show the probability; show why. "This concept has appeared in 7 of the last 10 board exams."
