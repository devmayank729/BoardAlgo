The Saponification Protocol

Saponification is simply the hydrolysis of an ester with NaOH or KOH to give alcohol and sodium or potassium salt of the acid.

$$ \text{CH}_3\text{COOC}_2\text{H}_5 + \text{NaOH} \rightarrow \text{CH}_3\text{COONa} + \text{C}_2\text{H}_5\text{OH} $$

"Eating Sodium Produces Soap & Alcohol"

(Ester + Sodium Hydroxide $\rightarrow$ Soap + Alcohol)
